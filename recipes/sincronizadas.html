Sincronizadas with Salsa Verde

Description

Sincronizadas are a traditional Mexican dish made from flour tortillas typically layered with cheese, ham, and veggies.

Ingredients

  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 2-3 cups of shredded mozzarella
  • 1lb of low sodium ham
  • 2-3lbs of raw boneless chicken
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 8 medium corn tortillas
  • 10+ tomatillos
  • 1 cup of oil
  • 1/2 bottle of pre-made Salsa Verde
  • 1 jalapeno
