Sincronizadas with Salsa Verde

Description

Sincronizadas are a traditional Mexican dish made from flour tortillas typically layered with cheese, ham, and veggies.

Ingredients

  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 2-3 cups of shredded mozzarella
  • 1lb of low sodium ham
  • 2-3lbs of raw boneless chicken
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 8 medium corn tortillas
  • 10+ tomatillos
  • 1 cup of oil
  • 1/2 bottle of pre-made Salsa Verde
  • 1 jalapeno

Steps

  1. Boil the chicken in a pot with the garlic cloves, salt, and pepper until no longer pink.
  2. Take cloves out and save broth for later.
  3. Peel and wash tomatillos, put in a pan with oil and jalapeno. Fry until tender and dark.
  4. Blend pre-made Salsa Verde, tomatillos,garlic, and 2 ladles of chicken broth.
  5. In the pot used to boil the chicken, add chicken, jalapeno, and blended Salsa Verde.
  6. Layer tortilla, cheese, ham, more cheese, and tortilla. Fry for about 2-3 minutes in an oiled pan.
  7. Plate one at a time while covering each one with sauce. Add chicken on the side.
